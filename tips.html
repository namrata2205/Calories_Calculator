Stay Fit & Healthy!

  • Drink at least 2–3 liters of water daily.
  • Include fruits, vegetables, and protein in every meal.
  • Exercise or walk for at least 30 minutes daily.
  • Avoid junk and processed foods.
  • Get 7–8 hours of good sleep every night.
